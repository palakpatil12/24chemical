<h1>Tall Oil Pitch (TOP) Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Tall Oil Pitch (TOP) is a byproduct derived from the distillation of crude tall oil, primarily obtained as a co-product of the kraft pulping process in the paper industry. It consists of a complex mixture of high-boiling organic compounds, including resin acids, fatty acids, and neutral compounds. Due to its adhesive and binding properties, TOP finds extensive applications across multiple industries, including asphalt additives, drilling muds, lubricants, and biofuel production.</p><p>
