<strong>Market Size</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Polyurethane resin for non-woven fabric is a specialized type of resin used in the manufacturing of non-woven textiles. These fabrics, which are created through methods such as bonding, felting, or spinning fibers together, benefit from polyurethane resin applications that enhance durability, flexibility, water resistance, and overall performance. This resin finds applications in various industries, including automotive, healthcare, filtration, and consumer goods, where non-woven fabrics are widely used for their lightweight and cost-effective properties.</p><p>
