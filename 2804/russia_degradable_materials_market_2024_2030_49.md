<strong>What Are Degradable Materials and Why Are They Important?</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Degradable materials are designed to break down naturally after use, reducing their long-term environmental impact. These materials, including biodegradable plastics, compostable packaging, and other eco-friendly alternatives, provide a solution to the rising concern over plastic waste and pollution. The need for degradable materials has gained momentum as consumers, governments, and industries push for more sustainable alternatives to traditional plastic products.</p><p>
