<strong>Market Size</strong></p><p>
</p><p>
</p><p>Galfan Steel is a type of steel coated with a zinc-aluminium alloy, commonly known as ZA. The coating, consisting of approximately 95% zinc and 5% aluminium, offers superior corrosion resistance and enhanced formability compared to traditional zinc-coated steel. </p><p>
</p><p>This makes Galfan Steel a preferred choice in industries such as construction, automotive, and infrastructure, where durability and long-term performance are critical. The unique properties of Galfan coating contribute to its widespread application in environments prone to harsh weather conditions and aggressive corrosion factors.</p><p>
