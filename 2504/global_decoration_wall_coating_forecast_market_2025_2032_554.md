<strong>Market Overview</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Decoration wall coatings refer to latex paints and wood coatings specifically designed for interior walls and wood surfaces in residential and commercial spaces. These coatings enhance the aesthetic appeal of interiors while providing protective features such as moisture resistance, durability, and ease of maintenance. The market includes a variety of products such as water-based paints, oil-based paints, and environmentally friendly coatings, catering to the growing demand for sustainable and high-performance decorative solutions.</p><p>
