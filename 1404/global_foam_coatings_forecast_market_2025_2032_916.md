<strong>Market Size</strong></p><p>
</p><p>
</p><p>Foam coatings refer to specialized protective layers applied to foam materials, enhancing their durability, aesthetic appeal, and functional characteristics. These coatings are commonly polymer-modified and fiber-reinforced, with a base composition of Portland cement. Foam coatings are extensively used for rigid insulation panels, foam shapes, and insulated concrete form systems. Typical coating materials include latex, PVC, acrylics, vinyl, activated carbon, and conductive carbon, each offering unique benefits such as weather resistance, improved strength, and thermal insulation.</p><p>
