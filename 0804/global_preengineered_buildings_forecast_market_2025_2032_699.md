</p><p>Pre-engineered buildings (PEBs) are a form of construction where building components such as beams, slabs, columns, and external walls are prefabricated and transported to the site for assembly. This method of construction is known for its efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and sustainability. PEBs are widely used in commercial, industrial, and residential applications due to their quick installation process and reduced waste generation.</p><p>

<strong>Drivers</strong></p><p>
