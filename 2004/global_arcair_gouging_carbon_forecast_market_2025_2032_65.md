</p><p>Arcair gouging carbon electrodes are specially designed for the air carbon arc metal removal process. This process melts metal using an electric arc and removes it with a jet of compressed air. The electrodes contain a precisely formulated blend of carbon and graphite, ensuring high metal removal efficiency, arc stability, uniform diameter, and clean, slag-free grooves or cuts. These electrodes are widely used in industries such as shipbuilding, automotive, aerospace, and heavy metal fabrication for cutting, gouging, and defect removal operations.</p><p>
