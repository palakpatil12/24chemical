<h1>Rigid Polyurethane Foam (RPUF) Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Rigid Polyurethane Foam (RPUF) is a closed-cell plastic widely used as a thermal insulation material in construction, refrigeration, and industrial applications. It is available in various forms, such as insulation boards, block foam, and sandwich panels, offering superior thermal resistance and structural integrity. RPUF is also used in spray foam applications for on-site insulation and energy-efficient building solutions.</p><p>
