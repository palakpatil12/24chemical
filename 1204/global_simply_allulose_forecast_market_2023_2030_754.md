Simply Allulose has 70% the sweetness of granulated sugar, but has almost no calories, so it can be used as a low-calorie sweetener. Simply Allulose has the advantages of high sweetness, good solubility, low calorie and low blood sugar response, and is considered as one of the most ideal sucrose substitutes. Adding Simply Allulose to food can not only improve its gelling degree, but also improve its flavor through Maillard reaction with food protein. Compared with D-fructose and D-glucose, Simply Allulose can generate more antioxidant Maillard reaction products and maintain the antioxidant level of food for a longer time.</p><p>
