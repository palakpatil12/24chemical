<strong>Market Size</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Potassium thioglycolate is a chemical compound widely used in the cosmetic industry for hair removal creams, perming solutions, and hair dyeing products. It acts as a powerful reducing agent, breaking down keratin bonds in hair, allowing for effective depilation and hair restructuring. Additionally, potassium thioglycolate finds applications in non-cosmetic sectors, such as culture media preparation and mineral processing, due to its selective inhibiting properties in copper-sulfur ores.</p><p>
