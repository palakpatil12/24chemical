<h1>Plant Oil Polyols Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Plant Oil Polyols are bio-based polyols derived from renewable plant sources such as soy, castor, palm, and canola oils. These polyols serve as essential raw materials in the production of polyurethane foams, coatings, adhesives, and sealants. Compared to petroleum-based polyols, plant oil polyols offer environmental benefits such as reduced carbon footprint, biodegradability, and sustainability, making them an attractive option in various industries, including automotive, furniture, construction, and packaging.</p><p>
