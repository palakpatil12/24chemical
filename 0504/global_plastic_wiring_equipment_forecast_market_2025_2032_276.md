</p><p>Plastic wiring equipment refers to the plastic auxiliary components used in electrical installations. These products facilitate the proper organization, safety, and efficiency of wiring systems. Key products in this category include nylon cable ties, steel nail wire clamps, wiring troughs, crimping caps, nylon fixing heads, expansion tubes, and number tubes. These components play a critical role in structuring complete wiring systems that meet regulatory and safety requirements.</p><p>
