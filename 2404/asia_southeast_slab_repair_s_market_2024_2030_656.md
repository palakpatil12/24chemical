In 2023, total consumption of slab repair products in Southeast Asia reached approximately 85,000 metric tons, with Singapore, Malaysia, and Indonesia accounting for 70% of regional demand. The infrastructure sector remains the largest consumer, representing 50% of demand, followed by commercial construction at 30% and residential at 15%.
