<h1>Potato Pregel Starch Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Potato Pregel Starch is a type of pregelatinized starch derived from potatoes. It is created by cooking and then drying starch in a controlled process, typically using a drum dryer or extruder, making it soluble in cold water. This starch modification enhances its functional properties, making it a valuable ingredient in various industries, including food processing, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and paper manufacturing.</p><p>
</p><p>Its applications range from use as a thickening agent in soups and sauces to its role as a binding agent in tablets and capsules.
