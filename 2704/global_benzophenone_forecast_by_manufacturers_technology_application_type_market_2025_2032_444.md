<h1>Benzophenone Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032, by Manufacturers, Regions, Technology, Application, Product Type</h1><p>Benzophenone is an organic compound primarily used as a UV filter in various industries, including cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and food packaging. It functions by absorbing UV radiation and preventing degradation caused by exposure to sunlight. Due to its high effectiveness in protecting against harmful UV rays, Benzophenone plays a crucial role in sun protection products such as sunscreens and tanning oils. Additionally, it is utilized as a stabilizer in food packaging to extend the shelf life of products and prevent UV-induced damage.</p><p>
