</p><p>Citric Acid Anhydrous is an organic acid that serves as a pH-control agent and flavor enhancer in a variety of products. It is a naturally occurring fruit acid, commercially produced through microbial fermentation of a carbohydrate substrate. Its non-toxic and biodegradable nature makes it a preferred choice across food, pharmaceutical, and cleaning industries. It appears as colorless crystals or white crystalline powder with a strong acidic taste, highly soluble in water, and chemically stable at ambient temperatures.</p><p>
