</p><p> <strong>Definition</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Flash spinning is a specialized process used primarily in the production of nonwoven fabrics. This technique involves the rapid evaporation of a solvent from a polymer solution, resulting in the formation of fine fibrils that bond together to create a strong, durable, and breathable fabric. The process is widely used in industries that require lightweight yet highly resistant materials, such as protective clothing, filtration, and insulation.</p><p>
