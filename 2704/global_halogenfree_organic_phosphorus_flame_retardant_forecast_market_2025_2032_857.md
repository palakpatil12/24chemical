</p><p>Halogen-free organic phosphorus flame retardants are chemical compounds designed to reduce the flammability of materials without using halogens such as chlorine or bromine. These flame retardants offer advantages like low smoke emission, non-toxicity, and environmental sustainability. They primarily include phosphates, phosphonates, phosphites, and organic phosphorus salts, which are widely utilized in industries such as electronics, automotive, textiles, and construction.</p><p>
